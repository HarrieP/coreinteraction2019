Reading #9 - "Designing Programmes" - Karl Gerstner

Topics/Questions

1. Gerstner believes that grid is a fundamental principle in design that plays a major role in 60s Swiss design. His famous "mobile grid" developed for the magazine Capital seemed amazing but at first glance, I had no idea how it worked.

2. He also explains about integral typography. According to him, the definition of integral is "shaped into a whole". He says that "letters are the elementary particles of the written language". Those could be figurative signs for sounds without content. He then shows the example of the combination of the four letters from a word "wife".
