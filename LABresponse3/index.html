Answers for the questions posed in each article's title

1. Rasheed believes that collecting is important since it leads a person through the process of attempting to reach an answer or a resolution. She was interested in "validating all forms of research" and the act of collecting helped her creating her own archive. Accoridng to her, collecting is a process of scanning everything and thinking about accessibiltiy of objects.

2. Rutherford Chang likes to work with materials that has "cultural meanings" that people are familiar with alreday. He started collecting particular objects that have the value. He attempted to rearrange them in order to reveal something more about their nature.

3. Jeremy Keith loves the Internet Archive since it collect literally everything; except for the fact that it is too centralised. He likes the idea of "owing your content and syndicating it out to social networks". According to Keith, collecting is priceless because anything that are digital can be preserved forever.
