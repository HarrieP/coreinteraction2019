Reading #7 - "Can Jokes Bring Down The Government" - Metahaven

Topics/Questions

1. Claims as political weapons against jokes are convincing, but sometimes jokes become part of the structure they are trying to interfere with.

2. As a totalization of economic and political formation, capitalism normalizes itself to the point that all resistance can be successfully folded within the grid of knowledge. The jokes and memes are one of the only ways out of the spell of capitalism's realism, as they are often conveyed by their very nature as nonsense.
